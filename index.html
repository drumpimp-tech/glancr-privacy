Privacy Policy — GLANCR Feed

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

GLANCR Feed does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Almost everything stays on your device.

Data Stored Locally On Your Device Only

Platform login sessions, app preferences, caption history, and any credentials you enter in Settings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to us.

Data Sent to Third Parties

If you use the AI caption generator, the prompt text you type is sent to our Supabase backend which forwards it to the Anthropic Claude API. No account information or personal identifiers are included.

Microphone Access

GLANCR requests microphone access for Voice Control — saying a platform name opens it fullscreen. Speech recognition is performed using Apple's on-device Speech framework. No audio is stored or transmitted. You can disable this in Settings.

Analytics and Tracking

GLANCR does not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, tracking pixels, or third-party cookies.

Third-Party Platforms

When you access social media platforms through GLANCR you are subject to each platform's own privacy policy. GLANCR has no control over their data practices.

Children's Privacy

GLANCR is rated 17+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 17.

Data Retention

We do not collect personal data on our servers. All local app data can be removed by deleting GLANCR from your device.
