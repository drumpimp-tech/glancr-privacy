# Privacy Policy — GLANCR

Last updated: April 2, 2026

---

## Overview

GLANCR ("the App", "we", "our") is a social media aggregator developed by Trevor Lawrence. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

The short version: **GLANCR does not collect, sell, or share your personal data.** Almost everything stays on your device.

---

## What We Collect

### Data Stored Locally On Your Device Only
- **Platform login sessions** — When you log into Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or any other platform inside GLANCR, your session cookies are stored locally on your device in the standard iOS WebKit data store — exactly the same way Safari stores them. We never see, access, or transmit these credentials.
- **App preferences** — Your layout choice, frame style, color palette, selected platforms, and tile order are stored locally in your device's UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.
- **Caption history** — Captions you save are stored locally on your device only.
- **Username/password reference** — Any credentials you optionally enter in Settings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted anywhere.

### Data Sent to Third Parties
- **Caption prompts** — If you use the AI caption generator, the prompt text you type is sent to our Supabase backend, which forwards it to the Anthropic Claude API to generate a caption. No account information, platform credentials, or personal identifiers are included.
- **Platform content** — When you load a platform's feed, your request goes directly to that platform's servers (e.g., tiktok.com, instagram.com). GLANCR acts as a browser — we are not involved in that communication.

---

## Microphone Access

GLANCR requests microphone access to power Voice Control — a feature that lets you say a platform name (e.g., "TikTok") to open it fullscreen.

- Speech recognition is performed using Apple's on-device Speech framework.
- Audio is processed locally. No audio recordings are stored or transmitted.
- You can disable this feature at any time in GLANCR Settings → Voice Control.

---

## Analytics and Tracking

GLANCR does not use:
- Analytics SDKs (Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
- Advertising networks or tracking pixels
- Crash reporting services that transmit personal data
- Third-party cookies

---

## Third-Party Platforms

When you use GLANCR to access social media platforms, you are subject to each platform's own privacy policy. GLANCR has no control over and takes no responsibility for the data practices of Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X, Facebook, or any other platform you access through the app.

---

## Children's Privacy

GLANCR is rated 17+ on the App Store due to unrestricted web access. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 17.

---

## Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to retain or delete. All local app data can be removed by deleting GLANCR from your device.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Updates will be reflected in the App Store listing and in the app itself. Continued use of GLANCR after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
